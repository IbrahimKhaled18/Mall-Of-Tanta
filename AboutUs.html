Welcome to Mall of Tanta, the premier shopping destination in Tanta, Egypt.

Our mall offers an unparalleled shopping experience, featuring a wide range of stores, restaurants, and entertainment options. Our mall boasts a vast array of stores, including international and local brands, catering to all your shopping needs. From fashion and beauty to electronics and home goods, we have everything you need under one roof. With over 200 stores, you can be sure to find what you're looking for. In addition to shopping, we offer a variety of dining options to satisfy all your cravings. Whether you're in the mood for a quick bite or a sit-down meal, we have something for everyone. From fast food to fine dining, our restaurants offer a diverse range of cuisines to suit all tastes. At Mall of Tanta, we understand the importance of entertainment, which is why we provide a range of options to keep the whole family entertained. From a state-of-the-art cinema to an indoor amusement park, we offer a variety of activities to keep you entertained for hours. We take pride in providing a comfortable and convenient shopping experience for our visitors. Our mall is equipped with ample parking, making it easy to access. Additionally, we offer a range of services, including free Wi-Fi, ATMs, and currency exchange, to ensure your visit is stress-free. We believe that Mall of Tanta is more than just a shopping destination, it's a community hub. We host a range of events throughout the year, from live music to holiday celebrations, bringing the community together. We hope you'll come and visit us soon and experience everything that Mall of Tanta has to offer.
